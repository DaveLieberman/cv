Image processing example of my work: Resolution enhancement

Resolution enhancement, 4X as many DICOM slices

The image deck on the left is the original sqquence of dicom images, and the enhanced spatial resolution version on the right,
has 4 times as many slices, reducing slice thickness from 2mm to 0.5mm. A compelling characteristic of this algorithm, is that if
4 consencutive slices are averaged, then the enhanced version precisly reproduces the low resolution original. Therefore, the high
resolution version is an actual feasible solution.
